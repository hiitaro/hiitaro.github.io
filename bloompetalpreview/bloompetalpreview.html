Our Story

A passion for living beauty

Every bouquet we create is a small act of love. I started Bloom & Petal from a tiny studio with a pair of scissors, a bucket of fresh roses, and an unstoppable belief that flowers can change the feeling of a room — and a heart.

"I don't just arrange flowers. I arrange feelings."

Today, each stem is hand-selected at dawn from local growers, and each design is composed with the same devotion as the very first bouquet I ever made.

— Isabelle Laurent, Founder
