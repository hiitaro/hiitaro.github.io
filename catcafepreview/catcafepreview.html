Our Story

A Sanctuary in the City

Whiskers & Coffee was born from a simple belief: that every rescue cat deserves a lounge, not a cage, and every coffee lover deserves a sanctuary. We have partnered with local Hollywood animal rescues to provide a "foster-lounge" environment.

Step inside and leave the bustle of Florida life behind. Our space is meticulously designed to be a "living room away from home," featuring warm oak textures, soft ambient lighting, and plenty of cozy nooks for both humans and cats.
